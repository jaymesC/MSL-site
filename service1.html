SERVICES

SCAFFOLDING: FORM WORKS, SALES AND SUPPLIES

HOPE QUEST HOT, DIPPED, SEAMLESS,4MM X 6.2M GALVANISED SCAFFOLDING TUBES.

MSL scaffolding tubes are galvanized to prevent rust and corrosion caused by weather factors and wear and tear. This also guarantees a longer life for the tubes.

SCAFFOLDING WOODEN BOARDS

MSL offers different sizes of BS2482 scaffolding wooden boards. They range from 4 metres, 3 metres to 2 metres. We provide these sizes to suit the needs of our clients.

SCAFFOLDING BASE JACK/ADJUSTABLE BASE JACK

We offer base jacks of BS4360/43A, BS1139 AND EN74 standards.
The base jack levels the scaffold tower when the tower legs are placed on surfaces of different elevations. It ensures all the legs will have (approximately) equal bearing on the substrate(s) the tower rests upon.

GIN WHEEL (BS851692)

Our gin wheel is 10mm in diameter and it is a great pulley wheel used to pull materials from the ground to the top of the scaffold structure.

SCAFFOLDING LADDER ACCESS GATE (BS 14122-3)

This is a safety essential when the access to the scaffold area is a ladder

LADDER ACCESS HATCH (BS14122-3)

The safety hatch is made of metal and is visible once attached to the access gate.
It closes off access to the ladder when not in use and helps prevent falls accidents.

DROP FORGED DOUBLE CLAMP

MSL drop forged double clamps are manufactured to BS/EN 74 & BS1139.
Used for connecting two 48.3mm tubes at right angles, they are zinc plated for long lasting life

DROP FORGED SWIVEL CLAMP

MSL drop forged swivel clamps are manufactured to BS/EN 74 & BS1139 and are tested to meet the quality control standards.

PRESSED STEEL SLEEVE COUPLER

MSL pressed steel sleeve couplers are manufactured to BS/EN 74 & BS1139 and are tested to meet the quality control standards.
This fitting is used for all scaffolding construction works.

DROP FORGED BEAM CLAMP (GRAVELOCK /GIRDER)

All MSL drop froged beam clamps are manufactured to BS/EN 74 & BS1139 and are tested to meet the quality control standards.
They are zinc plated for a longer lasting life.

DROP FORGED BOARD RETAINING COUPLER

All MSL drop froged board retaining couplers are manufactured to BS1139 and are tested to meet the quality control standards

DROPPED FORGED SINGLE/PUTLOG CLAMP

MSL Dropped Forged Single Clamps are manufactured to BS1139, zinc plated for longer lasting life and are tested to meet the quality control standards.

SCAFFOLDING CLAMP BAG

Our heavy duty, tough scaffolding bag is tough woven and carries load worth 50kg.

CASTOR WHEEL (750 TONS)

MSL Castor Wheels come with a brake pedal for safety and ease of use

GALVANISED LADDER BEAM

Our Galvanized Ladder Beams come in standard lengths of 2.4m, 3m, 4.8m and 6.3m.
They provide a clear span between scaffolds and are galvanized for a longer lasting life.

SCAFFOLDING TUBES END PROTECTOR

The safety of our clients is important to us. This tube end protector is made of rubber to help protect your workers from scraping and injuring themselves from the ends of the scaffolding tubes.
They are also made to BS/EN Standard.

SCAFFOLDING TAGS & HOLDER

MSL Scaffolding tags come in the standard green and yellow colors with a holder.

SCAFFOLDING FIBRE GLASS LADDER

Our Fibre Glass Ladders are 3 & 4 metres long and are non-conductive and chemical resistant.
Its orange color helps to keep workers alert and marks out the work area.

BAND AND PLATE

Our Bands and Plates are manufactured to BS1139 and are tested to meet the highest level of quality control.
