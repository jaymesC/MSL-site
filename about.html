ABOUT US

ABOUT US

MBARIE SERVICES NIG. LTD (MSL) is a multi-dimensional servicing company, providing services in the area of: Scaffolding form works, Sales and Supplies of scaffolding materials and accessories, Blasting and Painting equipment supplies and application, Procurement / Supply chain services,Pipeline Installation / Repairs Services, Supply of vessels and crew, Marine Support Services and Labour Resource Management.
We are OEM representatives for special materials, MSL’S capabilities in those core business areas have helped support the onshore and offshore oil industry by bringing together employees and technical expertise in related fields across the world. This has resulted in MSL recognized standing within the Nigerian Oil and Gas community as a leading provider of service solution within these areas of business.
Mbarie Services Nig Ltd is committed to providing our clients with quality products and services among which are scaffolding equipment, form works and accessories, Sandblasting, Painting Equipment and accessories. We are representative to world major OEM LAYHER i.e. GRACO & LAYHER SAS

Our Vision


To be a world class provider of quality goods and support services.

Our Mission


To become a high quality reliable company that will serve as a reference point for quality, skill, commitment, experience and expertise to the construction/manufacturing industry in Nigeria. Rendering services to our clients at an affordable cost that would enable us break even in the long term. Also to be the choice company in our area of specialization for clients, employees, and key suppliers in every industry market we serve.

Quality Policy

Mbarie Services Nigeria Limited, as an organization that provides construction, procurement /supply, equipment leasing and Engineering Support services, hereby express for itself this policy statement which is aimed at establishing the organization among the elite organizations in this part of the world.

It is the policy of MBARIE SERVICES NIG. LIMITED to provide products and services that satisfy the Customers’ needs, meets specified quality requirements and is in accordance with contractual, code and regulatory requirements.

Management shall ensure that objectives are set on annual basis both at the strategic level and at various functions and levels. Objectives shall be set during the last management reviews of the year for the successive year. Deliberate efforts shall be made to involve the employees and other stakeholders during policy formulation.

Mbarie Services Nigeria Limited is committed to satisfying all requirements, including legal requirements and other requirements to which the organization subscribes.

The organization is also committed to continually improving its Quality Management System through a proper implementation of its processes.

Management of Mbarie Services Nigeria shall ensure proper implementation of this policy.

EXPERIENCE
